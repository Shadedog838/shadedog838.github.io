Information About me

Hi, I’m Shandon Mith! I’m a software developer with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the Rochester Institute of Technology, where I graduated with a 3.6 GPA. During my time at RIT, I explored a wide range of subjects—from software engineering and database management to machine learning and data mining—while actively applying those skills in real-world development roles.

With over 1.5 years of hands-on industry experience, I’ve built and maintained full-stack applications using modern tools and frameworks. At FTI Consulting, I developed features using Laravel, Angular, and React, contributing to responsive and accessible client solutions. At UKG, I enhanced functionality and stability in the Dimensions app using Java Spring Boot and Angular, and I even built an AI-driven Teams chatbot during a company hackathon. While working at Emerson, I created APIs and SQL integrations for the RIDGIDConnect platform using .NET and Vue. I also helped Nielsen deploy its “Around the World” application by building server-side scripts and containerizing their Apache app with Docker.

Technically, my strengths lie in backend development with Java, C#, and Spring Boot, alongside solid experience in full-stack environments using React, Angular, and Node.js. I'm well-versed in database systems like MySQL, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB, and I regularly use tools like Git, Docker, and Kubernetes in my workflow.

When I’m not coding, I unwind by playing piano, diving into video games, and studying new languages—both spoken and scripted. I believe in solving tough problems with persistence, creativity, and a collaborative mindset.

I’m always eager to connect with fellow developers, recruiters, and tech enthusiasts. Whether it’s a deep dive into full-stack development or a casual conversation about the future of software, I’m all ears. Let’s connect and explore what we can build together!
